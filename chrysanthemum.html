Chrysanthemums are the birth flower of people born in November. They are also the national flower of Japan and are depicted in the emblem of the Japanese monarchy, the Chrysanthemum Throne.

Chrysanthemums are said to be the favored flower of the goddess of happiness and joy in Greek mythology. They symbolize optimism, joy, and longevity.
